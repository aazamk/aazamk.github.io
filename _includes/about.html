About

I am a Software Developer with a strong sense of design. My goal is to use technologies to design and develop applications that are useful, beautiful and easy to maintain.


During my 7-year adventure with android development, I had the opportunity to work with many tools. I like solutions that are simple yet powerful. My favourite toolbox nowadays is Kotlin, Databinding, JetPackCompose, Architecture components(LiveData, ViewModel, Room, WorkManager etc.), Coroutines, Dependency injection, Espresso, and Mockito.


My professional and personal life are guided by the principle "Keep it simple".
